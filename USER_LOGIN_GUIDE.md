# User Login Guide

## For Individual Users

This guide is for employees/users who want to check their own transaction history.

## How to Login

### Step 1: Get Your Credentials
Ask your admin for:
- Your registered name
- Your registered phone number

### Step 2: Open the App
Go to the BMS Cash Entry app login page.

### Step 3: Enter Your Details
- **Username**: Enter your full name (exactly as registered)
- **Password**: Enter your phone number (exactly as registered)

### Example:
If you are registered as:
- Name: "Rajesh Kumar"
- Phone: "9876543210"

Then login with:
- Username: `rajesh kumar` (case doesn't matter)
- Password: `9876543210`

### Step 4: Click Login
You'll be taken directly to your transaction history page.

---

## What You Can See

After logging in, you'll see:

### Your Information
- Your name
- Your phone number
- Current month and year

### Your Transactions
- Date and time of each transaction
- Purpose (Food, Fuel, Advance, etc.)
- Amount given
- Filter by purpose (All, Food, Fuel, Advance, Others)

### Monthly Total
- Total amount given to you this month
- Displayed at the bottom in a green card

### Actions You Can Take
- ✓ View all your transactions
- ✓ Filter transactions by purpose
- ✓ Download PDF of your transactions
- ✓ Share your transactions via WhatsApp

---

## What You CANNOT See

For privacy and security:
- ✗ Other users' transactions
- ✗ Global transaction history
- ✗ Salary payment records
- ✗ Deleted transactions
- ✗ Home screen with all buttons

---

## Features Available to You

### 1. Filter Transactions
- Click the dropdown at the top
- Select: All, Food, Fuel, Advance, or Others
- View only transactions of that type

### 2. Download PDF
- Click "📄 Download PDF" button
- Get a professional PDF with:
  - Company header and logo
  - Your name and phone
  - All your transactions in a table
  - Monthly total

### 3. Share via WhatsApp
- Click "📱 WhatsApp" button
- Share your transaction summary
- Includes all transaction details
- Formatted for easy reading

## Logging Out

### To Logout:
1. Click the back arrow (←) at the top
2. You'll be logged out automatically
3. Taken back to login screen

**Note:** There's no home screen for users - back button = logout

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?

**Check your credentials:**
- Is your name spelled correctly?
- Is your phone number correct?
- Names are case-insensitive (JOHN = john = John)
- Phone numbers must match exactly

**Common Issues:**
- Extra spaces in name
- Wrong phone number
- Not registered in system yet

**Solution:**
- Ask admin to verify your registration
- Check exact spelling of your name
- Confirm your phone number

### Don't See All Transactions?

**Check the filter:**
- Make sure "All Transactions" is selected
- Not filtered to specific purpose

### Can't Download PDF?

**Check browser:**
- Allow pop-ups for this site
- Check downloads folder
- Try a different browser

### Session Expired?

**If logged out automatically:**
- Just login again
- Your session is saved
- Will remember you next time

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data is Private
- You can only see YOUR transactions
- Other users cannot see your data
- Only admins can see all transactions

### Session Security
- Your session is saved in browser
- Stays logged in until you logout
- Automatic logout on back button

### What Admins Can See
Admins can:
- See all users' transactions
- Add transactions for you
- Delete transactions (with reason)
- Save salary payments

## Need Help?

### Contact Your Admin
If you:
- Can't login
- See wrong transactions
- Need to update your details
- Have questions about amounts

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my password?**
A: Your password is your phone number. Ask admin to update it.

**Q: Can I see last month's transactions?**
A: Currently shows current month only. Ask admin for historical data.

**Q: Why can't I add transactions?**
A: Only admins can add transactions. This is for security.

**Q: Can I delete a transaction?**
A: No, only admins can delete. Report errors to admin.

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: Real-time. Refresh page to see latest transactions.
